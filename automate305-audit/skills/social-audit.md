# Skill: Social Media Audit

## Purpose
Diagnose a prospect's social media at the system level — not just "post better."
Most businesses don't have a content problem. They have a SYSTEM problem.
This audit reveals which of the 4 operating levels they're at and exactly what it takes to level up.

---

## The 4 Levels of Social Media Operations

Every prospect falls into one of these. Identify their level first — it shapes the entire pitch.

| Level | Score | What It Looks Like |
|-------|-------|-------------------|
| **1 — Chaos** | 0-30 | Posts when they remember. Random content. No visual brand. Low engagement. "We should post more." |
| **2 — Manual Consistency** | 31-55 | Posts 3-4x/week but manually. Content decent but not strategic. Engagement doesn't convert. Exhausting to maintain. |
| **3 — System-Driven** | 56-75 | Content calendar exists. Visuals are consistent. Some scheduling. But still mostly human-run, no AI layer. |
| **4 — AI-Powered** | 76-100 | Content batched + AI-assisted. Repurposing automated. Data-driven. Runs without daily attention. (This is what AUTOMATE305 builds) |

**The pitch:** Show them where they are → show them Level 4 → show them we're the bridge.

## Step 1 — Profile & First Impression (Before You Scroll)

Open their profile cold. First 10 seconds. Answer:

**Profile Picture:**
- [ ] Real face / recognizable logo? (personal brands must use a face)
- [ ] High resolution? Clear?
- Score: Clear & professional (+2) / Acceptable (+1) / Weak or missing (0)

**Bio:**
- [ ] Does it say WHO they help + WHAT result they get + HOW to take action?
- [ ] Is there a link? Where does it go? Does the destination match the CTA?
- [ ] Does it use the 150 characters efficiently or waste space on filler?
- Score: Complete and compelling (+2) / Partial (+1) / Vague or missing (0)

**Highlights:**
- [ ] Do they use Instagram Story Highlights?
- [ ] Are Highlights named strategically (Testimonials, Results, FAQ, About, Offers)?
- [ ] Are covers branded/consistent?
- Score: Strategic and branded (+2) / Exists but messy (+1) / None or irrelevant (0)

**Link-in-Bio:**
- [ ] Is there a link? (If no: automatic -2)
- [ ] Does it go to a landing page, lead magnet, or booking page?
- [ ] Or does it go to a generic homepage? (Wasted opportunity)
- Score: Strategic destination (+2) / Homepage (+1) / No link (0)

**Profile Section Score: ___/8**

## Step 2 — The 9-Grid Audit (Last 9 Posts)

Look at their last 9 posts as a grid. This is how every new follower sees them.

**Visual Consistency:**
- Do the 9 posts feel like they belong to the same brand? (Colors, fonts, style)
- Is there a visual system or is it random?
- Score: Cohesive (+3) / Somewhat consistent (+2) / Random (+0)

**Content Mix Analysis:**
Categorize each of the 9 posts:
- **E** = Educational (teaches something)
- **P** = Proof (results, testimonials, case studies)
- **O** = Offer (promotes their service/product)
- **L** = Lifestyle/Culture (humanizes the brand, builds connection)
- **R** = Repurposed (Reel, carousel, etc.)

Ideal mix for service businesses: ~40% E / 30% P / 20% O / 10% L
Red flags: All O (pure sales) or all L (no business purpose) or all E (no proof or offer)

**Record their actual mix:** E:___ P:___ O:___ L:___ (out of 9)

Score: Balanced strategic mix (+3) / Some strategy (+2) / Random or single-type (+0)

**Post Format Mix:**
- [ ] Are they using Reels? (Algorithm heavily favors Reels for reach in 2025)
- [ ] Carousels? (Highest save rate → signals algorithm to push content)
- [ ] Static posts only? (Lowest reach)
- Score: Reels + Carousels (+3) / Mix with some Reels (+2) / Static only (+0)

**Grid Score: ___/9**

## Step 3 — Hook Quality Test (Last 5 Reels/Videos)

The hook is everything. First 1-3 seconds = stay or scroll.

For each of their last 5 Reels, read the first line of the caption and/or the text on screen.
Ask: Would this stop a cold scroll?

**Weak hook patterns (common failures):**
- Starts with their name or brand: "At [Business], we believe..."
- Starts with "I" without tension: "I wanted to share something..."
- Generic opener: "Tips for better skin..." / "Here are 5 ways to..."
- No text on screen for first 3 seconds

**Strong hook patterns:**
- Contrarian: "Stop doing X" / "X is a lie"
- Specific curiosity gap: "Most [ICP] don't know this about X"
- Bold claim: "This one change added $X to my business"
- Emotional trigger: "I almost quit before this happened"
- Direct address: "If you're a [ICP] in [city], watch this"

**Score each hook:** Strong (+2) / Acceptable (+1) / Weak (0)
**Hook Quality Score: ___/10**

## Step 4 — Engagement Rate Analysis

**How to calculate:**
For their last 12 posts: (Total likes + comments) ÷ Followers × 100 = Engagement Rate %

**Industry benchmarks (2025):**
| Account Size | Good ER | Average ER | Poor ER |
|-------------|---------|-----------|---------|
| Under 5K | 8-15% | 4-7% | Under 3% |
| 5K-20K | 4-8% | 2-4% | Under 2% |
| 20K-100K | 2-4% | 1-2% | Under 1% |
| 100K+ | 1-2% | 0.5-1% | Under 0.5% |

**Their followers:** ___
**Their avg engagement rate:** ___%
**Benchmark for their size:** ___%

**Engagement quality check (go beyond the number):**
- [ ] Are comments genuine or just emojis?
- [ ] Is there back-and-forth in comments? (Community vs broadcast)
- [ ] Do they reply to comments within 24 hours?
- [ ] Do they have saves and shares? (Higher signal than likes)

**Engagement Score:** Above benchmark (+4) / At benchmark (+2) / Below (+0)

---

## Step 5 — Content Strategy & Funnel Check

Social media without a funnel is entertainment, not marketing.

**Does their content serve a business funnel?**
- [ ] Does content regularly drive people to take an action (link in bio, DM them, etc.)?
- [ ] Is there a clear CTA in captions? (Or does every post just end with nothing?)
- [ ] Do they have any "lead generation" content? (Giveaways, "DM me for X", free resource CTAs)
- Score: Clear funnel strategy (+3) / Occasional CTA (+2) / No funnel (+0)

**Posting Frequency & Consistency:**
- [ ] How many posts/week over the last 30 days? (Count it)
- [ ] Any gaps of 7+ days? (Red flag)
- 4+ posts/week consistently (+3) / 2-3/week (+2) / 1/week or less (+0)

**Stories:**
- [ ] Are they using Stories daily? (Stories = relationship + top-of-mind)
- [ ] Do Stories have CTAs? (Polls, links, "DM me" stickers)
- [ ] Ratio of face/talking-head Stories vs static image reposts?
- Active daily Stories (+3) / Occasional (+2) / Inactive (0)

**Strategy Score: ___/9**

## Step 6 — Miami-Specific Opportunity Scan

This is the local edge that national agencies miss. Check for:

**Bilingual Content Opportunity:**
- [ ] Is their audience Miami/South Florida? (Check geo tags on posts)
- [ ] Are they posting any Spanish or Spanglish content?
- [ ] Is their bio bilingual?
- Miami reality: ~70% of Miami-Dade is Hispanic. English-only = leaving half the market untouched.

**Local Hashtag Usage:**
Check their recent posts for local tags like:
`#Miami` `#MiamiBusiness` `#Miami305` `#MiamEntrepreneur` `#MiamiLife` `#Brickell` `#CoralGables` `#WynwoodArt` `#LittleHavana` — and industry-specific local tags

- [ ] Are they using any Miami-specific hashtags?
- [ ] Are they tagging Miami locations?
- Score note: Not scored but flagged as opportunity in report

**Community Engagement:**
- [ ] Are they engaging with other Miami local accounts?
- [ ] Have they done any local collabs, shoutouts, or features?
- [ ] Are they tagged in other Miami accounts' content?

**Cultural Moment Alignment:**
Miami has a calendar of high-engagement moments. Are they leveraging:
- Art Basel (December) — biggest cultural event
- Calle Ocho Festival
- Miami Open / F1 Miami Grand Prix
- Miami Spice (restaurant season)
- Carnaval Miami
- Local sports (Heat, Marlins, Inter Miami)

**Local Opportunity Score:** Flag high / medium / low (not included in main score)

## Step 7 — Cross-Platform Check (2 minutes)

**TikTok (if they have it):**
- [ ] Same content as Instagram or native TikTok content?
- [ ] Posting frequency vs IG
- [ ] Engagement comparison

**LinkedIn (if B2B):**
- [ ] Profile complete? (Headline, about, featured section)
- [ ] Are they posting company updates?
- [ ] Are decision-makers in their industry on LinkedIn?

**Facebook:**
- [ ] Still active? (Most local businesses still get leads here, especially 35-55 age group)
- [ ] Business page vs personal?
- [ ] Reviews visible and recent?

## Operating Level Diagnosis

Based on their final score:

**Level 1 — Chaos (0-10/20):**
> "Your social media isn't working because there's no system behind it. Right now it's 100% dependent on someone having time and energy to post. That's not a content strategy — that's hoping for the best. We build the system so it runs on autopilot."

**Level 2 — Manual Consistency (11-14/20):**
> "You're doing the right things, but it's costing you way too much time. You're manually doing what we can automate. The gap between where you are and Level 4 is a system, not more effort."

**Level 3 — System-Driven (15-17/20):**
> "You've built something real. The gap now is the AI layer — content repurposing, automated distribution, data-driven optimization. You're doing in 10 hours what we could do in 2."

**Level 4 — AI-Powered (18-20/20):**
> "Honestly, your social is in good shape. Let's talk about where the bigger gaps are — [redirect to their lowest scoring pillar]."
