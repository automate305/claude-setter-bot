# Skill: Automation Opportunity Scan

## Purpose
Identify where the prospect is doing manual work that AUTOMATE305 could automate — and quantify the time/money cost of NOT automating it.

This is our core differentiator. Every business we audit has automation holes. Finding them is what makes us sound like we already understand their operation.

---

## Input Needed
- Business name, industry, website
- Any info from the prospect about their current process (ask these questions)

---

## The 5 Questions to Ask (or Infer)

If you're talking to the prospect directly, ask these in a casual conversation:

1. "When someone reaches out to inquire about your services, what happens next — do you get an email, a DM? Who handles it?"
2. "If someone doesn't book right away, how do you follow up with them?"
3. "How do you handle appointment scheduling? Do people book online or do you go back and forth?"
4. "What marketing tasks eat the most time for you or your team every week?"
5. "Are you sending regular emails to your past clients or leads? If so, how do you do that?"

If you can't ask directly, infer from their website and social presence.

---

## The 10 Automation Opportunities to Check

### 1. Lead Intake Automation
**Manual version:** Prospect fills out contact form → owner gets email → owner replies manually
**Automated version:** Form → CRM entry → immediate personalized email reply → task assigned to team
**Time saved:** 2-5 hours/week for active businesses
**Tool:** Make.com + CRM (HubSpot, GHL, Airtable)

**How to identify:** Do they have a contact form? Is there any indication of a CRM? Look for "Powered by [CRM]" in page source.

---

### 2. Lead Follow-Up Sequences
**Manual version:** Lead goes cold → owner tries to remember to follow up → usually doesn't
**Automated version:** Lead enters → 5-7 email/SMS sequence fires automatically over 14 days
**Time saved:** 3-8 hours/week + significantly higher conversion
**Tool:** Make.com + email platform (Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp)

---

### 3. Appointment Booking
**Manual version:** "What time works for you?" back-and-forth over DM/email
**Automated version:** Calendly/Cal.com link → auto confirmation → reminder sequences
**Time saved:** 1-3 hours/week
**Tool:** Calendly + Make.com

---

### 4. Content Scheduling & Repurposing
**Manual version:** Owner sits down and posts to each platform manually
**Automated version:** Create once → auto-distribute to IG, FB, LinkedIn, TikTok on schedule
**Time saved:** 4-8 hours/week
**Tool:** Buffer/Later + Make.com for repurposing

---

### 5. Review/Testimonial Collection
**Manual version:** Manually ask clients for reviews, never follows through consistently
**Automated version:** Post-service trigger → automated review request → positive reviews to Google, negative caught first
**Time saved:** 1-2 hours/week + compounding brand value
**Tool:** Make.com + email/SMS trigger

---

### 6. Client Onboarding
**Manual version:** New client → owner manually sends contract, invoice, onboarding docs, schedules kickoff
**Automated version:** Signed contract → auto invoice → onboarding packet → kickoff scheduled, all in sequence
**Time saved:** 3-6 hours per new client
**Tool:** Make.com + Docusign/HelloSign + payment processor

---

### 7. DM / Inquiry Response
**Manual version:** DMs pile up, some go unanswered, leads fall through
**Automated version:** Keyword triggers → ManyChat or IG automation → immediate response + lead capture
**Time saved:** Daily interruptions eliminated
**Tool:** ManyChat, IG DM automation

---

### 8. Email Newsletter / Campaign
**Manual version:** Rarely sends emails, does it manually when they remember
**Automated version:** Weekly email sends automatically based on content calendar, personalized by segment
**Time saved:** 2-4 hours/week + consistent list nurture
**Tool:** ActiveCampaign/Klaviyo + Make.com

---

### 9. Reporting & Analytics
**Manual version:** Logs into 5 different platforms to cobble together monthly numbers
**Automated version:** Single dashboard with all metrics — social, email, ads, revenue — updated automatically
**Time saved:** 2-4 hours/month + better decisions
**Tool:** Make.com + Google Data Studio / Airtable dashboard

---

### 10. Re-Engagement Campaigns
**Manual version:** Old leads never contacted again after initial quote
**Automated version:** 90/180-day re-engagement sequence fires automatically for cold leads
**Time saved:** Mostly missed revenue recovered — not time saved
**Tool:** Make.com + CRM tags + email

## Quantifying the Cost of Manual Work

Use this when writing the report to translate gaps into money:

| Manual Task | Avg Time/Week | Rate Equivalent | Annual Cost of Doing Manually |
|-------------|--------------|-----------------|-------------------------------|
| Lead intake + reply | 3 hrs | $25/hr | $3,900/yr |
| Follow-up (manual) | 5 hrs | $25/hr | $6,500/yr |
| Social posting | 6 hrs | $25/hr | $7,800/yr |
| Reporting | 3 hrs/mo | $25/hr | $900/yr |
| Client onboarding | 4 hrs/client × 2/mo | $50/hr | $4,800/yr |

**Standard stat to use:** "On average, businesses that automate lead follow-up see 2-3x higher conversion on the same number of leads."
