## The 5-Pillar Audit Framework

Every audit scores the prospect across 5 dimensions (0-100 each):

1. **Website & Messaging** — Is the homepage clear? Does the CTA convert? Is the offer obvious in 5 seconds?
2. **Social Presence** — Are they consistent? Engaging? Is the content serving a funnel or just vibes?
3. **Lead Generation** — Do they have a lead magnet? Email capture? Any kind of funnel?
4. **Automation Readiness** — What are they doing manually that should be automated?
5. **AI Opportunity Score** — Where could AI save them 5-20 hours/month?

Total score out of 500. Anything under 300 is a strong pitch opportunity.

## Session Workflow

### Audit Session Start
1. `git pull` to get latest prospect-tracker.md
2. Get prospect's: company name, website URL, Instagram/social handle, industry
3. Run the 5-pillar audit using skills/ files
4. Fill in report-template.md with findings
5. Present report to prospect
6. Pitch retainer using retainer-pitch.md

### Audit Session End
1. Update prospect-tracker.md with outcome
2. Commit and push: `git commit -m "Audit — [CompanyName] — [date] — [outcome]"`
