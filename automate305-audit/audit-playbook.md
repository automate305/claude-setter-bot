# AUTOMATE305 — Audit Playbook

## Overview

This is the step-by-step methodology for running a free audit. Takes 15-25 minutes with AI assistance. Produces a report the prospect actually reads and remembers.

The audit has 5 pillars. Score each 0-20 (max 20 per pillar = 100 total). Under 60 total = strong retainer opportunity.

---

## Before You Start

Collect from the prospect (or find yourself):
- [ ] Business name
- [ ] Website URL
- [ ] Instagram handle (or primary social)
- [ ] Industry / what they sell
- [ ] Rough monthly revenue or team size (if known)
- [ ] Current biggest marketing pain point (ask them directly)

---

## Pillar 1 — Website & Messaging (0-20)

**Goal:** Determine if the homepage converts a cold visitor in under 10 seconds.

**Check these in order:**

1. **Above-the-fold clarity** — Can you tell WHAT they do and WHO it's for in 5 seconds?
   - Clear: +4 / Vague: +1 / Confusing: 0

2. **Primary CTA** — Is there one clear next step? (Book a call, Buy now, Get started)
   - Single clear CTA: +4 / Multiple competing CTAs: +2 / No CTA: 0

3. **Social proof** — Testimonials, results, logos, reviews visible without scrolling?
   - Strong proof: +4 / Weak/buried proof: +2 / None: 0

4. **Mobile experience** — Does the site load fast and look clean on mobile?
   - Great: +4 / Acceptable: +2 / Broken: 0

5. **Offer clarity** — Is the actual product/service and price (or range) easy to find?
   - Clear: +4 / Hidden: +2 / Nonexistent: 0

**Score: ___/20**

**Key finding to report:** What is the single biggest website gap costing them leads?

---

## Pillar 2 — Social Presence (0-20)

**Goal:** Determine if their social media is building or burning trust.

**Check these:**

1. **Posting consistency** — Are they posting at least 3x/week on their primary platform?
   - Consistent: +4 / Sporadic: +2 / Abandoned: 0

2. **Content quality** — Does the content look professional and on-brand?
   - High quality: +4 / Mixed: +2 / Low quality: 0

3. **Engagement rate** — Comments, saves, shares (not just likes)
   - Active community: +4 / Some engagement: +2 / Ghost town: 0

4. **Content strategy** — Is content serving a funnel (value → CTA → offer) or just random?
   - Clear strategy: +4 / Random: +2 / No strategy: 0

5. **Bio & link optimization** — Does the bio clearly say what they do + link to offer/lead magnet?
   - Optimized: +4 / Partial: +2 / Weak: 0

**Score: ___/20**

**Key finding to report:** What's the biggest social gap?

---

## Pillar 3 — Lead Generation (0-20)

**Goal:** Determine if they have any system for capturing and nurturing leads.

**Check these:**

1. **Lead magnet** — Do they offer something free in exchange for contact info?
   - Yes, clear and relevant: +4 / Yes but weak: +2 / No: 0

2. **Email capture** — Is there an email opt-in on the site? Is it prominent?
   - Prominent: +4 / Buried: +2 / None: 0

3. **Email marketing** — Are they sending regular emails to their list?
   - Regular (1x+/week): +4 / Occasional: +2 / None: 0

4. **Follow-up sequence** — Is there an automated email sequence for new leads?
   - Yes: +4 / Partial: +2 / No: 0

5. **CRM / lead tracking** — Are leads tracked anywhere? (CRM, spreadsheet, anything)
   - Yes, organized: +4 / Partial: +2 / No: 0

**Score: ___/20**

**Key finding to report:** How much revenue is leaking from untracked/unnurtured leads?

---

## Pillar 4 — Automation Readiness (0-20)

**Goal:** Identify manual processes that should be automated.

**Probe questions to ask or infer:**

1. **Lead intake** — Are new inquiries handled manually (email replies, DMs)?
   - Fully automated: +4 / Partially: +2 / 100% manual: 0

2. **Follow-up** — Are follow-ups tracked and sent manually?
   - Automated: +4 / Some tracking: +2 / All manual: 0

3. **Appointment booking** — Do they use Calendly or similar, or schedule via DM/phone?
   - Self-serve booking: +4 / Partial: +2 / All manual: 0

4. **Reporting** — Are marketing results tracked automatically?
   - Dashboard/automated: +4 / Manual tracking: +2 / No tracking: 0

5. **Content distribution** — Is content scheduled or posted manually each time?
   - Scheduled/batched: +4 / Mostly manual: +2 / All manual: 0

**Score: ___/20**

**Key finding to report:** How many hours/week are being lost to manual tasks we could automate?

---

## Pillar 5 — AI Opportunity Score (0-20)

**Goal:** Identify where AI tools could give them the biggest leverage.

**Look for these gaps:**

1. **Content creation** — Are they struggling to create enough content consistently?
   - Not an issue: +4 / Somewhat: +2 / Major bottleneck: 0 (flip = opportunity)

   > Note: Low score here = HIGH opportunity for AUTOMATE305

2. **Customer communication** — Are DMs, emails, inquiries taking too much manual time?
   - Automated: +4 / Some: +2 / All manual: 0

3. **Ad copy & creative** — Are they running ads? Is creative tested/refreshed regularly?
   - Yes, optimized: +4 / Running but stale: +2 / No ads: 0

4. **Competitive intelligence** — Do they track competitors systematically?
   - Yes: +4 / Occasionally: +2 / Never: 0

5. **Data & reporting** — Are they making decisions based on data or gut feel?
   - Data-driven: +4 / Mixed: +2 / All gut: 0

**Score: ___/20**

---

## Scoring Guide

| Total Score | What It Means | Pitch Approach |
|-------------|--------------|----------------|
| 80-100 | They're doing well. Not an ideal retainer candidate right now | Compliment, offer a specific upgrade, plant a seed |
| 60-79 | Solid foundation but clear gaps. Good retainer opportunity | Show the 2-3 biggest gaps and exactly how we'd fix them |
| 40-59 | Multiple critical gaps. Strong opportunity | Paint the picture of what the business looks like with systems in place |
| Under 40 | Significant room to grow. Easiest close | Be direct: "You're leaving serious money on the table. Here's the plan." |

---

## After Scoring: Write the Report

Use `report-template.md` to generate the client-facing output. Fill in:
- Their scores per pillar
- The top 3 specific findings (with examples from their actual site/content)
- The "What We'd Do" section — 3 specific things AUTOMATE305 would fix immediately
- The retainer recommendation

Then use `retainer-pitch.md` to present it.
